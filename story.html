WHO ARE YOU?

CALLBAK FOCUS ON STORYTELLING

Callbak is a narrative development company focused on international co-production.
We advocate for premium minimalism, using a streamlined production approach that balances artistry with practicality.

In collaboration with production companies we focus on creating original, high-quality content in a market flooded with formulaic films,
audiences are increasingly seeking well-crafted stories that challenges and engages them.

With technology democratised, the rise of AI and film innovation opening new doors,
Callbak emphasises the importance of compelling, human-centered narratives over technical spectacle.
